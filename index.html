About

Nestled amidst the lush green slopes of Munnar, Misty Highlands is a sanctuary for travelers seeking peace, comfort, and a deeper connection with nature. Surrounded by endless tea plantations and cool mountain air, our resort blends elegant living with the raw beauty of Kerala's highlands. Wake up to golden sunrises painting the mist-covered hills, spend your afternoons immersed in the soothing sounds of nature, and unwind in the evening as the sky transforms into a canvas of soft mountain hues. Every corner of Misty Highlands is thoughtfully designed to complement the serene landscape, offering spacious rooms, panoramic balconies, and warm hospitality that makes you feel instantly at home.

Our architecture reflects a harmonious blend of contemporary comfort and traditional charm, ensuring that modern amenities coexist seamlessly with the natural surroundings. Whether you choose to relax by the infinity pool, indulge in rejuvenating spa therapies, or explore nearby trails and tea estates, your stay promises a perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. Whether you are planning a romantic escape, a family holiday, or a quiet retreat from city life, every moment here is designed to help you slow down, breathe deeply, and rediscover tranquility. At Misty Highlands, we don't just offer a stay — we offer an experience that lingers long after you leave.

Activities

🌄 Sunrise Trek

Start your day with a guided trek through mist-covered hills and rolling tea plantations. As the sun rises over the mountains, enjoy breathtaking panoramic views and refreshing mountain air.
🕒 Duration: 2 - 3 Hours
📊 Difficulty: Moderate
👥 Suitable For: Couples, Nature Lovers, Adventure Seekers

🚙 Jeep Safari

Explore rugged terrains and hidden forest trails in an exciting off-road jeep safari. Discover scenic viewpoints and untouched landscapes while enjoying a thrilling ride.
🕒 Duration: 1.5 - 2 Hours
📊 Difficulty: Easy
👥 Suitable For: Families, Groups, All Age Categories

🍃 Tea Walk

Take a peaceful stroll through lush tea gardens and learn about the journey of tea from leaf to cup. Experience the aroma of fresh leaves and interact with local plantation workers.
🕒 Duration: 1 - 1.5 Hours
📊 Difficulty: Easy
👥 Suitable For: Families, Senior Guests, Relaxation Seekers

🔥 Campfire Night

Unwind under the starlit sky with a cozy campfire experience. Enjoy light music, storytelling, snacks, and the warmth of the fire in the cool mountain breeze.
🕒 Duration: 2 Hours (Evening)
📊 Difficulty: Relaxed
👥 Suitable For: Couples, Families, Friends
